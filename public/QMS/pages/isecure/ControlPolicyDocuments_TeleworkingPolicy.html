Teleworking Policy
ISMS Clause / Control: A 6.3.2 ICPo3 Version: 1.00 Date: 10th April, 2017

3.0 Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this documented information is to provide an overview of the various policies. The required policies to be adhered in all processes preserve the Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability of the information system of the organization. Applicable to all activities, personnel performing the activities, personnel responsible for the activity to be performed in the defined manner covering the scope of activities at organization.

3.4. Procedure

3.4.1. General

The policy for information security defined below has been approved by management for publication and communication to employees along with relevant external parties.

3.4.2.1. The Teleworking policy will be as defined in the NDA given by HR.

3.4.2.2. This policy is applicable to processes and process owners who work from offsite location of the organization, and access organization's resources.

3.4.2.3. Any Exemptions to this policy is allowed, where there is a business need to be exempted from this policy (since it is too costly, too complex, adversely impacting other business requirements) a risk assessment must be conducted being authorized by ISMS MR.

3.4.2.4. All remote access is allowed only with authentication.

3.4.2.5. When accessing the network from a laptop, authorized users are responsible for preventing access to any organization's computer resources or data by non-authorized users and adhere to the Mobile device policy of the organization.

3.4.2.6. Performance of illegal activities through the organization's network and access systems by any user (authorized or otherwise) is prohibited. Please refer to Information transfer policy, Mobile device policy and BYOD policy also for more info.

3.4.2.7. The authorized user bears responsibility for and consequences of misuse of the authorized user's access.

3.4.2.8. Authorized users will not use organization's resources for outside business interests.

3.4.2.9. Authorized users shall protect their login and password, even from family members.

3.4.2.10. Authorized users should ensure the confidentiality of information is preserved, when accessing organization's resources in public spaces.

3.4.2.11. Organization's email must never be used in local / public web browsing places.

3.4.2.12. No organization information can be transferred via pen drive / CD when working in offsite location, unless permitted to do so with privilege rights granted to users by ISMS MR.
