1.0 Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this documented information is to provide an overview of the various policies. The required policies to be adhered in all processes preserve the Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability of the information system of the organization. 1.4.1. General

1.4.2. Mobile Device Policy

1.4.2.1. Any Exemptions to this policy is allowed, where there is a business need to be exempted from this policy (since it is too costly, too complex, adversely impacting other business requirements) a risk assessment must be conducted being authorized by ISMS MR.

1.4.2.2. This policy is applicable to all Mobile devices, issued by organization and owned by users or issued and owned by organization.

1.4.2.3. The use of mobile devices while driving vehicle is not advised by the organization.

1.4.2.4. All Users must be trained to ensure the proper use of organization issued devices and corporate resources on the mobile devices.

1.4.2.5. All Mobile devices that carry organization information or belongs to the organization must be protected by password access to that device including mobile application if any, accessing company information.

1.4.2.6. The user has to protect the device against damage, loss and theft.

1.4.2.7. Users to ensure that the unauthorized access to the device must be avoided.

1.4.2.8. While in the office, the network access to the device must be controlled by the IT department. Apart from those devices managed by IT, devices are not allowed to be connected directly to the internal corporate network.

1.4.2.9. Only organization approved applications can be installed on such devices, and can be done only by the IT department.

1.4.2.10. Any changes in security configurations must be carried out only by IT department.

1.4.2.11. In case of damage, loss or theft of device, users must report AS SOON AS POSSIBLE to the IT department, in order to take the appropriate measures

1.4.2.12. All approved Mobile devices shall be tagged for its approval identification.

1.4.2.13. Digital camera embedded on mobile devices must not be used for photography purposes in organization's sensitive areas or on organization confidential information, unless authorized to do so.

1.4.2.14. Photography via mobile devices is strictly prohibited in the organization for employee's and third parties, unless written approval or authorization from ISMS MR is obtained.

1.4.2.15. Device's firmware must be up-to-date; Firmware patching and updating processes are the responsibility of the IT department.

1.4.2.16. Mobile devices MUST have antivirus software installed to prevent viruses.

1.4.2.17. If a user suspect that unauthorized access to organization data has taken place via a mobile device, then user must report the same with organization's incident handling process.

1.4.2.18. Devices must not be ''jail broken/rooted'' or have any software/firmware installed which is designed to gain access to functionality not intended to be exposed to the user.

1.4.2.19. Users must not load pirated software or illegal content onto their devices.

1.4.2.20. Any mobile device connected to internal networks, should be authorized to do so, and logs of such connectivity should be traceable
