7.0 Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this documented information is to provide an overview of the various policies. The required policies to be adhered in all processes preserve the Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability of the information system of the organization. Applicable to all activities, personnel performing the activities, personnel responsible for the activity to be performed in the defined manner covering the scope of activities at organization.

7.4. Procedure

7.4.1. General

The policy for information security defined below has been approved by management for publication and communication to employees along with relevant external parties.

7.4.2. Review of user access rights policy

7.4.2.1. Privilege rights & authentication credentials provided to any user must be reviewed every 6 months once, or upon change of role, whichever is earlier or on termination.

7.4.2.2. Logs of such review must be maintained in the organization.

7.4.2.3. Upon termination of the role, the access rights of an individual to information and assets associated with information processing facilities and services must be removed or suspended

7.4.2.4. If a departing employee or external party user has known passwords for user IDs remaining active, these must be changed upon termination or change of employment, contract or agreement.

7.4.2.5. Arrangements must be made to advise all other employees and external party users involved to no longer share this information with the person departing.
