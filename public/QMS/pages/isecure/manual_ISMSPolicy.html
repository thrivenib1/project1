Integra Information Security Policy:

Integra is committed to safeguard the Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability (CIA) of information in all forms (physical and electronic) by protecting the identified information assets of the organization. This shall be achieved as described in the table below:

PROTECTION Satisfy the applicable internal, external and statutory requirements related to information security and to review the same at regular intervals
CONTROLS Establish controls for protection of the organization's information systems
REGULATION STANDARDS Comply with ISO/IEC 27001 International Standards for Information Security (IS) and establish the IS objectives for continual improvement
COMMUNICATION Communicate the importance of IS to all individuals and entities in a business relationship within and external to the organization
