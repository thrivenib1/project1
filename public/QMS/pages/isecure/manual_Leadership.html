5.0 Leadership

5.1 Leadership and commitment

Top management of the organisation demonstrates its leadership and commitment with respect to the information security management system by:

  1. ensuring the establishment of information security policy and the information security objectives that are compatible with the strategic direction of the organization;
  2. ensuring the integration of the information security management system requirements into the organization's processes and day to day work related activities;
  3. ensuring that the resources needed for the information security management system are available;
  4. communicating the importance of effective information security management and of conforming to the information security management system requirements;
  5. ensuring that the information security management system achieves its intended outcome(s);
  6. directing and supporting persons to contribute to the effectiveness of the information security management system;
  7. promoting continual improvement; and
  8. supporting other relevant management roles to demonstrate their leadership as it applies to their areas of responsibility.

The MD of the organisation, show complete commitment in achieving all the above descriptions.

5.2 Policy

Top management of the organisation has established the information security policy, and prefaced it in this Manual, the established policy:

  1. is appropriate to the purpose of the organization;
  2. includes information security objectives or provides the framework for setting information security objectives;
  3. includes a commitment to satisfy applicable requirements related to information security; and
  4. includes a commitment to continual improvement of the information security management system.

The information security policy is:

  1. Available as documented information;
  2. Communicated within the organization; and
  3. Available to interested parties, as appropriate.

5.3 Organizational roles, responsibilities and authorities

Top management of the organisation ensures that the responsibilities and authorities for roles relevant to information security are assigned and communicated.

Top management has assigned an ISMS Management Representative, Mr. Bhaskar Jyoti Phukan, member of the senior management of the Organisation, with the responsibility and authority for:

  1. ensuring that the information security management system conforms to the requirements of this International Standard; and
  2. reporting on the performance of the information security management system to top management.

To ensure the effective implementation of the ISMS in the organisation, the Top Management has also nominated an Information Security Core team, Core team is a management framework established to ensure management of information security within the organisation. The committee shall comprise of the organization's management representative, Functional Representatives and any Interested Parties (if required).
