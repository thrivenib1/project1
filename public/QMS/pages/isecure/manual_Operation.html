8.0 Operation

8.1 Operational Planning and Control

The organization plans, implements and controls the processes needed to meet information security requirements, and to implement the actions determined in 6.1 of ISO 27001:2013 std.

The organization also implements plans to achieve information security objectives determined in 6.2 of ISO 27001:2013 std.

The organization keeps documented information to the extent necessary to have confidence that the processes have been carried out as planned.

The organization controls planned changes and review the consequences of unintended changes, acting to mitigate any adverse effects, as necessary.

The organization ensures that outsourced processes are determined and controlled.

8.2 Information security risk assessment

The organization performs information security risk assessments at every 6 months once or when significant changes are proposed or occur, taking account of the criteria established in 6.1.2 a). The organization retains documented information of the results of the information security risk assessments.

Reference: Information security risk assessments and treatment Records.

8.3 Information security risk treatment

The organization implements the information security risk treatment plan.

The organization retains documented information of the results of the information security risk treatment.

Reference: Information security risk assessments and treatment Records.
