Disposal of Media Procedure
ISMS Clause / Control: A 8.3.2 ICPr4 Version: 1.00 Date: 10th April, 2017

4.0 Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this documented information is to provide an overview of the various policies. The required policies to be adhered in all processes preserve the Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability of the information system of the organization. Applicable to all activities, personnel performing the activities, personnel responsible for the activity to be performed in the defined manner covering the scope of activities at organization.

4.4. Procedure

4.4.1. General

The procedure for information security defined below has been approved by management for publication and communication to employees along with relevant external parties.

4.4.2. Disposal of Media

4.4.2.1. Reliable transport or couriers should be used for such transportation of media, if not ISMS core team member or a person nominated by core team member should personally carry out the activity.

4.4.2.2. Packaging of such media should be sufficient to protect the contents from any physical damage likely to arise during transit.

4.4.2.3. Logs should be kept, identifying the content of the media, the protection applied as well as recording the times of transfer to the transit custodians and receipt at the destination

4.4.2.4. Where media is being transported for recovery all external parties associated with such transportation and its receipt should enter an NDA / Confidentiality agreement between the organization and such sources should be approved by the organization prior to use of the service.

4.4.2.5. Media containing confidential information should be stored and disposed of securely based on the nature of media:

  • hardcopy: shredding / Incernation
  • softcopy: erasure of data in a non-recoverable manner.

4.4.2.6. Any media - asset that carried asset valuation of more than 3, must require secure disposal.

4.4.2.7. The ISMS core team member should monitor the entire disposal process.

4.4.2.8. Disposal of all media assets should be recorded in the media disposal register.

4.4.2.9. Only after issuance of Asset Disposal Note, any media asset belonging to the organization is allowed for disposal.
