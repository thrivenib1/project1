1.0 Scope

The scope of Information Security Management System for below 2 entities is defined as below:

a. Integra Micro Systems Pvt. Ltd.

To protect information and information assets related to activities and services of Integra Micro Systems (P) Ltd., including software development, testing, product maintenance, technical documentation, consulting, field operations, transaction processing, remote operations management, hardware development and sales/supply of integrated solutions.

b. Integra Micro Software Service Pvt. Ltd

To protect information and information assets related to activities and services of Integra Micro Software Services (P) Ltd., including software development, testing, product maintenance, technical documentation, consulting, resource augmentation and reselling of information technology products.

3.0 Exclusions

The following controls are excluded from the scope of the organisation activities; these exclusions do not affect organisation's ability, or responsibility, to provide products and services, that meet customer and applicable regulatory requirements:

a. Integra Micro Systems Pvt Ltd.

Controls Excluded Controls Description Justification of Exclusion
A.14.2.7 Outsourced development The organisation, has a complete internal software development team, and does not outsource any this activity, hence this control is excluded.

b. Integra Micro Software Services Pvt Ltd.

Controls Excluded Controls Description Justification of Exclusion
A.14.1.2 Securing application services on public networks Presently in the Organisation there are no application services passing over public networks and no application services transactions as the organisation is not involved in any ''electronic commerce'' and ''On-line transactions'', hence this control is excluded.
A.14.1.3 Protecting application services transactions Presently in the Organisation there are no application services passing over public networks and no application services transactions as the organisation is not involved in any ''electronic commerce'' and ''On-line transactions'', hence this control is excluded.
A.14.2.7 Outsourced development The organisation, has a complete internal software development team, and does not outsource any this activity, hence this control is excluded.
