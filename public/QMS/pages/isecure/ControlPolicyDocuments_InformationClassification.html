4.4.2. Information Classification Policy

4.4.2.1. In the context of information security, the classification of Information is done based on its level of sensitivity and the impact to the organization, should that information be disclosed, altered or destroyed without authorization.

4.4.2.2. This policy is applicable to all information's, created, received, referenced or accesses for the organization business purpose.

4.4.2.3. Any Exemptions to this policy is allowed, where there is a business need to be exempted from this policy (since it is too costly, too complex, adversely impacting other business requirements) a risk assessment must be conducted being authorized by ISMS MR.

4.4.2.4. The classification and labeling of information is on following basis, in correlation to the IS03_Annexure_1 :

  1. Organization Confidential - Labeling required
    • Confidentiality - High
    • Integrity - High
    • Availability - High
    • Examples: Legal or Regulatory based information's, Accounting Information, Customer Data, Salaries and other personnel data, Encryption Information's, etc.
  2. Department Internal - Labeling required
    • Confidentiality - Medium
    • Integrity - High
    • Availability - High
    • Examples: Department Data, Configuration backup's, Program code, test cases, test reports, etc.
  3. Organization Internal - Labeling required
    • Confidentiality - Medium
    • Integrity - Medium
    • Availability - Medium
    • Examples: Organization wide information, Organization Policies / Procedures, etc.
  4. Unclassified - Labeling not required
    • Confidentiality - Low
    • Integrity - Low
    • Availability - Low
    • Examples: Publicly available data, Website data, etc.

    4.4.2.5. Classification of Information done should be clearly labeled on all the documents and sharing of the classified information should comply with the Information Transfer Policy.

    4.4.2.6. All External personnel / organization, should sign an NDA / Confidentiality Agreement, prior to exposure on any information to the company.
