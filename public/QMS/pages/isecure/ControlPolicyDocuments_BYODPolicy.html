2.0 Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this documented information is to provide an overview of the various policies. The required policies to be adhered in all processes preserve the Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability of the information system of the organization. Applicable to all activities, personnel performing the activities, personnel responsible for the activity to be performed in the defined manner covering the scope of activities at organization.

2.4.1. General

The policy for information security defined below has been approved by management for publication and communication to employees along with relevant external parties.

2.4.2. BYOD Policy

2.4.2.1. Any Exemptions to this policy is allowed, where there is a business need to be exempted from this policy (since it is too costly, too complex, adversely impacting other business requirements) a risk assessment must be conducted being authorized by ISMS MR.

2.4.2.2. This policy is applicable to all own mobile devices, that is used to access or contain the information of organization wherein the user has signed a BYOD User Acknowledgment and Agreement with the IT department, before use of the devices in the Organization.

2.4.2.3. When approved, Employees may use their mobile device to access the following Organization-owned resources: o Email user o Calendars o Contacts o Documents

2.4.2.4. Any violation of IT act of India / subjected province, the device user acknowledges bearing the complete liability arising from the caused violation.

2.4.2.5. Link for IT act: http://www.cyberpolicebangalore.nic.in/IT-Act.html

2.4.2.6. The device must lock itself with a password or PIN if it's idle for 1 minute.

2.4.2.7. Rooted (Android) or jail broken (iOS) or pirated windows devices are strictly forbidden from accessing the network and Organization's information.

2.4.2.8. Smartphone's and tablets BYODs that are not authorized by the organization are not allowed to connect to the network.
