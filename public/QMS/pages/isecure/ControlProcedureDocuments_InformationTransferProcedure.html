Plan for Risk - 1
Risk Documents Lost � Electronic (in large numbers) / Storage Server Corruption or Loss
Probability Low
Impact to organization High
Likely Scenario Server crash
Likely Threat Likely Vulnerability
1. Unable to access data 1. Lack of immediate restoration of data
2. Service disruptions 2. Lack of good anti malware systems
3. Loss of data / information 3. Improper management of Technical Faults
Impact to CIA Confidentiality Yes Moderate - 2 Integrity Yes High - 3 Availability Yes High - 3
Functions Affected All electronic and paper-based document related activities
Action Immediately:
Report Incident immediately to IT Team / ISMS MR, with high priority for recovery.
IT Manager to advise all affected departments / units about the management of problem.
If problem cannot be fixed within one day by recreation from backup, discuss with all stakeholders and take the decision for storage of data in alternate form, in access controlled local PC's to ensure continuity of operations.
If problem cannot be fixed by recreation from backup, investigate ways and need to recreate from paper files, or from individual staff members or supplier's documents etc �involve all stakeholders
Responsibilities Senior staff member to coordinate and escalate to Top Management if required
