11.0 Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this documented information is to provide an overview of the various policies. The required policies to be adhered in all processes preserve the Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability of the information system of the organization. Applicable to all activities, personnel performing the activities, personnel responsible for the activity to be performed in the defined manner covering the scope of activities at organization.

11.4.2. Working in Secure Areas Policy

An exemption to this policy is allowed, where there is a business need to be exempted from this policy (since it is too costly, too complex, adversely impacting other business requirements) a risk assessment must be conducted being authorized by ISMS MR.

11.4.2.1. This policy is applicable to all working areas covered in the scope of the certification of the organization.

11.4.2.2. Physical access control must be imposed to all working areas.

11.4.2.3. Vacant secure areas will be physically locked and periodically checked.

11.4.2.4. Computer screens should be angled away from the view of unauthorized persons.

11.4.2.5. All Storage areas are under protection systems with lock and key or security monitoring.

CCTV must be available for coverage to ensure non-repudiation of any activity in the work areas of the company.

11.4.2.6. Area's which are contractually required to be secured for preservation of information security; such areas are controlled via access cards to prevent unauthorized personnel presence in working areas.

11.4.2.7. Visitor's and third party personnel are discouraged in such areas, however if needed, any visitor or third party personnel unless agreed with security agreements should always be accompanied by the organization personnel into such areas.

11.4.2.8. Third party personnel are strictly prohibited to take photography or photocopies of any information that is available in secured work areas of the organizations.

11.4.2.9. All visitors must always wear the ID, while in the premises.

11.4.2.10. Glass Panels, if used in cladding / walls of room of work area's they must be designed in a manner to prevent information exposure to unauthorized personnel.

11.4.2.11. Unsupervised working in secure areas should be avoided both for safety reasons and to prevent opportunities for malicious activities;

11.4.2.12. Delivery and loading personnel should not access the secured working areas.
