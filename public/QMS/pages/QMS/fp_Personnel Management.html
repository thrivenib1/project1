Personnel Management
ISO Clause: 6.2 FP03 Version: 3.60 30th March 2015

3.1 Introdution

3.1.1 Purpose
The purpose of this procedure is to define a mechanism for Personnel Management.

3.1.2 Policy
This process will be applicable to the employees of the company for effective discharge of the human resource management activities as per the HR policy of the company.

3.1.3 Scope
This procedure will be applicable to the employees in Integra.

3.3 Task Details

3.3.1 Issue Appointment Letters

The Director / Head-Personnel /COO will issue the appointment letter to the candidate on completion of joining formalities by the Candidate. The Personnel In-Charge will induct the candidate as per the procedure explained in Recruitment / Staffing Policy of Integra Personnel Manual.

3.3.2 Create the Skill database entry in Talent Management Portal(TMP)

The Personnel In-Charge will ensure that whenever a new employee joins, the employee information is added in the skill database in TMP.

Personnel Team will update the skill database of the employee with data from the concerned PM/RAB Co-ordinator once in a quarter.

3.3.3 Provide Induction Training Requirements

The Personnel In-Charge will organise and coordinate for the Induction Training Program for the new Employees. The information of new employee will be provided to Quality Department for organising Quality Awareness Training.

3.3.4 Provide necessary Resources

Whenever a new employee joins, the Personnel department will send an e-mail request to System Administrator to allocate an e-mail id, and register finger print in Biometric attendance system. Arrange access card and ID card from General Admin In-Charge.

Provide details of new employee along with employee number, email ID, DOJ and designation to concern BU head.

3.3.5 Implementation of Performance Management and Feedback System

The Personnel In-Charge will ensure the implementation of Performance Appraisal System as per the Performance Appraisal Policy, explained in the Integra Personnel Manual. The Personnel In-Charge will ensure that all employees are appraised in a timely manner. Each employee must get performance feedback once a year.

3.3.6 Training Feedback Process:

The HR will collect the Training requests from the individual employee through the process of quarterly performance appraisal. HR will consolidate the requests and send to Talent Management department (training department) using the form FROTR08 facilitate respective trainings for the individual. Talent Management will arrange for the requested training after getting the approval (email) from reporting manager .After the completion of training, the Talent Management department will measure the effectiveness of training from employee and reporting manager using the forms FR0TR22 and FR0TR23 . The status of the training and effectiveness is reported back to HR by updating the form FR0TR08 and enclosing the filled forms (FR0TR22 and FRTR23) via email.The templates:

Form No Description Type
FROTR08 HR Training Request IN
FR0TR22 Trainee Training Effectiveness OUT
FR0TR23 Training Effectiveness by Manager OUT

3.3.7 Conduct Employee Satisfaction Survey

The Personnel In-Charge will identify the list of internal customers for survey. The survey will be done at least once in a year or more frequently on a need basis as decided by the Management.

The Personnel In-Charge will ensure that employee satisfaction survey is carried out using PCC /Web Based questionnaire.

The Personnel In-Charge will consolidate the survey responses and Management. will review the same and take necessary corrective and preventive action.

3.3.8 Accept a Resignation

The Reporting Head of the Employee will forward the Resignation of the Employee to Personnel In-Charge for necessary action.

3.3.9 Separation Logistics

The Head-Personnel or a senior management member identified will conduct the exit interview and give the feedback to the Personnel Dept. The Personnel Dept. will record the exit interview details.

Relieving Authorization must be obtained by COO/Director for the Employee Clearance. The Head of HR is authorized to sign all the relieving documents once Relieving Authorization is obtained.

An Employee Clearance FRHRR04 and is required for the human resource being released under separation, either through resignation or if the management terminates the service.

3.3.10 Maintain Personnel Records

The Personnel Dept. will maintain the following records for the employees:

  • Initial Resume
  • Candidate Profile
  • Candidate Evaluation Sheet (FRHR02)
  • Skill Data Sheet
  • Appointment Letter
  • Medical Examination Reports
  • Exit Interview Reports

3.3.11 Status Reporting

The Personnel In-Charge will report the status as below:

  1. Monthly Status Report to COO consisting resource statistics, recruitment, and attrition.
  2. Weekly Status Report to COO, BU Heads consisting of resource statistics, recruitment and utilisation.

3.3.12 Prepare Personnel Policies

The Personnel In-Charge will prepare and maintain personnel policies including the following policies:

Recruitment Staffing, Compensation and Benefits, Employee Benefits, Non-Disclosure Agreement, Personal Conduct, Working Hours, Leave, Travel, Medical Reimbursement, Performance Incentive, Performance Management, Promotion, Employee Concern Resolution, Employee Separation, Internet Access Policy, Transfer, Relocation etc.

The various policies will be compiled to form the "Integra Personnel Manual". The policies will be reviewed once in a year based on employee satisfaction survey and the industry trend.

3.3.13 Approve Personnel Policies

The new personnel policies or changes to any personnel policy will be reviewed and approved by the Director.

3.3.14 MLD and MLD

The personnel in-charge will maintain MLD and MLR for the function.

3.3.15 Metric Collection and Analysis

The Metrics pertaining to the function will be collected as per Metric Plan and analysed (Quarterly) for corrective / preventive action.

3.3.16 Retention and Disposition of Records

  • HR will retain and maintain all the personal records of the employees.
  • The Ex-employee files will be converted to soft copy and retain hard copies.
