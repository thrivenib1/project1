Recruitment
ISO Clause: 6.1 & 6.2 FP04 Version: 3.90 Date: 19th Oct, 2017

4.1 Introdution

4.1.1 Purpose
The purpose of this procedure is to define a mechanism for the recruitment function.

4.1.2 Policy
This process will be followed for recruiting employees to the organisation. The Managing Director/ COO will approve any process deviation in recruitment.

4.1.3 Scope
This procedure will be applicable in recruitment of employees to the organization.

4.3 Task Details

4.3.1 Prepare Manpower Plan

Based on the business plan prepared by the Director/COO, the Head - Recruitment will prepare and maintain a manpower plan for the month. The plan is prepared before start of the month and is reviewed and revalidated before planning for next month.

In the event the plan for manpower is not provided, HR should work on need basis request which will be raised using HR requests and RAB requirements which we receive from Enquiry Management System(EMS). The Director/COO approval for not having the plan must be maintained in records

4.3.2 Prepare Recruitment Plan

Based on the Manpower plan, Head Recruitment will prepare and maintain a Recruitment plan for the month and the plan is reviewed and revalidated monthly along with Business Unit Heads/Functional Heads

4.3.3 Process HR Requests from Management

The Recruitment Team will process the Human Resource Hiring Request (FRREC01) received from the Business Unit or Functional Heads and will update the Recruitment Plan accordingly. Hiring Requests are raised for the Human Resource Requirements, which are not covered in the manpower plan. The Recruitment team will monitor and close the requests and report the hiring status in their Monthly Status Report.

4.3.4 Sourcing of Candidates

  • Identification of resources will be as per the qualification criteria stated in the (Minimum requirement for various roles) Document No: HR-03. Any deviations have to be recorded along with an approval from concern BU.
  • RAB requirements sourcing will be done based on JD and HM-03 will not be applicable.

The sources can be the following:

Internal Source

  • Internal Job Posting -Intranet and /or E-mails
  • Employee Referral

External Sources

  • Recruitment through advertisements, social media.
  • Job Portals
  • Campus Recruitment for fresh graduates
  • Training Institutes
  • Networking and Search
  • Walk-ins/ Job fares
  • Direct applications to the recruitment department through the email id mentioned in the company website www.integramicroservices.com under career section
  • Manpower Consultants

4.3.5 Short-listing of Candidates

Candidates who respond to any of the recruitment modes mentioned above are expected to submit the hardcopy of their resumes. Candidates whose qualification, experience and skill sets are found matching with the requirements advertised are registered to participate in the recruitment process. The recruitment process for each role is defined in the Document HM-03.

The registered candidates for various roles would be put through tests as mentioned in the document HM-03. The details of the answer sheets, question papers and the answer keys to be used for the process of conducting tests are mentioned in the Master List of Documents.

The candidates are short listed for interviews based on the criteria defined in document HM-05.

Based on urgency of resources requirement - BU head can relax in short listing criteria.

Back ground verification has to conducted for making the offer for level 60 and above positions.

4.3.6 Organise and Conduct Interview

The candidates who are short listed for interviews would have to fill in a candidate profile FR 67A. Once the candidate fills the candidate profile he/she would have to undergo interview, which would be conducted in two rounds.

The first stage of the interview will be technical.

The second stage of the interview will be to assess the overall suitability of the person for the role and to fix the compensation and benefits.

The Head-Recruitment, in consultation with the BU Head, will identify interviewer(s) depending on the experience and skills of the candidate.

Interviews for the candidates are done in two rounds, one technical round and one HR round. Based on the need, second round technical will be conducted before HR round.

The selection of Interviewer will be done based on the post for which a candidate has applied and will be identified by BU head.

Post Round 1 Round 2 Closures
Software Engineer Software Engineer (with an exp. of at least 2 yrs) or person nominated by BU Head Team Lead and above (Round 2 Optional) or person nominated by BU Head BU Head / Head Recruitment
Sr. Software Engineer Team Lead and above or person nominated by BU Head PM / Architect/ DH /BU Head or person nominated by BU Head BU Head / Head Recruitment
Team Lead PM / Architect DH / BU Head BU Head / Head Recruitment
Architect / PM DH BU Head BU Head / Head Recruitment
Support Functions Functional head Functional head Head Recruitment

For any other post, Head Recruitment in Consultation with BU Head will identify the Interviewers.

The interviewer(s) will evaluate the candidate during the interview(s) and the results will be recorded in the Candidate Evaluation Sheet (FRHRR02).

Once a candidate has been cleared in the second stage of interview, the Head- Recruitment or a member of the recruitment team will explain the terms and conditions of employment and finalize the offer. The discussed offer would be recorded in the offer discussion sheet (HD-01).

4.3.7 Back to Back Offers for RAB requirements

Based on the approval from Business Heads to cater RAB requirements the recruitment for specific customer requirements will be processed as below:

  1. Suitable profiles will be shared with BU head and technical team to conduct technical telephonic/F2F interview.
  2. Shortlisted candidate profile will be uploaded in EMS by respective recruiter.
  3. Uploaded profiles will be converted to Integra format by coordinator and will be uploaded in EMS for further processing.

The short listing for these cases will be not followed as HM-03

4.3.8 Issue Offer Letters

Based on the outcome of the interview(s) an offer of appointment will be given to the selected candidate. The following documents have to be provided as annexure to the offer of appointment given to the candidate:

  1. Annexure listing the documents that are to be provided by the candidate while joining
  2. Salary Break details: A new appointment request is raised HR-05 and an approval is obtained on the new appointment request from the Director/COO/–.. An appointment order is made to the selected candidate after he/she joins the organisation and completes all joining formalities.

4.3.9 Status Reporting

The Head-Recruitment will report the monthly status as per Recruitment Monthly Status Report for all the HR requests.,

The Head-Recruitment will report weekly status on RAB requirement. Also, the open and closed reports on RAB recruitments will be available in EMS Portal. The access for reports will be available to COO, BU head and Sales Head.l

4.3.10 MLD and MLR

The Recruitment In-Charge will maintain MLD and MLR for the function.

4.3.11 Metric collection and analysis

The Metrics will be collected as per Metric Plan and analysed (quarterly) for corrective / preventive action.

4.3.12 Retention and Disposition of Records – (NC Report)

  • The owner of the record will maintain the records for a period of six months.
  • All the records which are over six months old will be shredded (using shredder) in HR cabin in the presence of HR head and the disposal data will accounted through mail of what documents are disposed.
