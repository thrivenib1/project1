Overview

Integra has been an ISO certified company since 2001. In this section, you will get the documented processes, compliant with the ISO standard.
This Quality Management System applies to the Design, Development and Maintenance of Software Products and Services handled by Integra.

The following are applicable standards and guidelines for the quality manual:

a) ISO 9001:2008
b) ISO 19011:2011
c) ISO 27001:2013
d) CMMI-Dev V 1.3
