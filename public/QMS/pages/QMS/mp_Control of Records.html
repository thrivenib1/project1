Control of Records
ISO Clause: 4.2.4 MP02 Version: 3.00 Date: 17th May 2010

2.1 Introdution

2.1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this procedure is to define the process of control and maintenance of records by the project team and functional area members.

2.1.2 Policy

All projects and functions will maintain the project records/ functional area records as stated in this process document for ease of operation and clarity.

2.3 Task Details

2.3.1 Maintenance of Records

Functional managers will define the structure of the folder for storing the records of the function.

The project Records will be kept in three Folders each Folder having various Sub-Folders under it where various records are kept, as mentioned below:

All the CI (Configuration Items) and project records will be kept in CVS. The PM will assign access rights to confidential documents/records in the project folder.

The three folders are:

· Project folder

· Quality folder

· SCM folder

The PM will control access to quality records during project execution. If contractually required, access to quality records will be provided to the customer for the agreed period.

2.3.2 Archival and disposal of Project Records

The PM / Function Head will submit the quality records for archival to the General Admin In-Charge. Please refer to Backup, Recovery and Archival procedure PR10 for further details.

Retention Period: The suggested retention period for the quality records is three years from the date of archival or till the end of warranty, whichever is earlier, unless otherwise stated by the customer.

Functional department will maintain the records for three years or as per statutory requirements.
