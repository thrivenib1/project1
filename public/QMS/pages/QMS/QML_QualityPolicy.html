Quality Policy

IMSS is firmly committed to business excellence in Software Development and Services by continually improving its quality system and meeting expectations of its internal and external customers.

The Objectives of the Quality System for the year 2017-18 are:

  • Limit Schedule Variance to 10% in software development & services.
  • Limit Effort Variance to 15% in software development & services.
  • Convert at least 10% of enquiries into orders.
  • Limit Escaped Defect Ratio to 5%.
  • Limit Project Cost Variance to 20%.
