Control of Customer Supplied Material
ISO Clause: 6.2 PR012 Version: 3.00 Date: 17th May 2010

12.0 Introdution

12.1.1 Purpose
The purpose of this procedure is to describe the mechanism for handling customer supplied material.

12.1.2 Policy
This policy will be followed for managing all customer supplied material for a project.

12.1.3 Scope
This procedure will be applicable to the following:

  • Documents
  • Source Code
  • Third Party Tools
  • Hardware

and any other items supplied by the customer.

12.1.4 Entry Criteria
Project kick-off.

12.2 Stakeholders

12.2.1 Responsibilities

Sl. No. Role Responsibilities Authority
1. PM Receive Customer Supplied Material Accept or reject the CSM
2. PL/PM/System Administrator Verify Customer Supplied Material NIL
3. PL/PM Label Customer Supplied Material NIL
4. PM, System Administrator Maintain Customer Supplied Material NIL
5. PM / Administration Officer Return/Disposition of Customer Supplied Material NIL

12.3 Task Details

12.3.1 Receive Customer Supplied Material
The PM will receive and list the Customer Supplied Material in the List of Customer Supplied Material (FRCSM01). The material can be received electronically or through courier.

12.3.2 Verify Customer Supplied Material

  • The PM should ensure verification of the Customer Supplied Material. Any damaged or unsuitable item received should be brought to the notice of the customer and the issue should be resolved.
  • All customer supplied material will be verified using Inward Material Inspection Checklist CHIMI01
  • The customer-supplied material will be reviewed as per the Verification (PR07) procedure.
  • In case of any discrepancy in the customer-supplied material, FRVER01 is submitted to the CPC for approval.
  • The Customer supplied document will be subject to the procedure for Control of Documents MP01
  • In case of code received from the customer, they will be responsible for the correctness, completeness and quality of the supplied code. The PM should ask for the test report of the same.

12.3.3 Label Customer Supplied Material
Labelling the item shall identify the hardware media containing third party tools and videotapes. The format of the label is given below:

Customer Supplied Material
Item Name:
Version:
Date Received:
Verified By:
  • The source code and third party tools received through e-mail or ftp will be included in the configuration baseline as per the Configuration Management procedure (PR04).
  • The documents supplied by the Customer will be identified as per the MP01 Control of Documents procedure.

12.3.4 Maintain Customer Supplied Material

  • The PM will maintain the included source code, documents and third party tools received through e-mail or ftp from the customer as per the Configuration Management procedure (PR04).
  • PM shall ensure use of commercial software supplied by the customer as per permitted site licenses.
  • The system administration team will maintain the hardware received from the customer as per the System Administration and Management (FP10) procedure.

12.3.5 Return Customer Supplied Material

  • The PM will return or dispose the Customer Supplied Material (CSM) as per the contractual agreement. The PM will update the items returned mentioned in the List of Customer Supplied Material (FRCSM01). If returned, the customer shall acknowledge receipt of the same. PM shall hand over all returnable CSM along with necessary documents to the Administrative Officer, who in turn shall send these items to the customer. PM shall verify with the customer to confirm the receipt of the items.
  • Non-returnable items shall be handed over to the system administration team along with the project archival during the Project Closure.
