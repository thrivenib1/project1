Risk Management
ISO Clause: 7.3.1 PR09 Version: 3.00 Date: 17th May 2010

9.0 Introdution

9.1.1 Purpose
The purpose of RSKM is to identify potential problems before they occur so that risk-handling activities can be planned and invoked as needed across the life of the product or project to mitigate adverse impacts on achieving objectives.

9.1.2 Policy
Establish Risk Management process for better (more accurate and proactive) planning and coordination of the projects. Organization need to have mitigation and contingency plans in place to achieve.

9.1.3 Scope
The scope of RSKM Process is applicable for all the projects and support functions in Integra.

9.1.4 Entry criteria

RSKM process is defined for all process & project in the organization, RSKM related activities are triggered by events Qualifying for this.

  • Triggers are specified in RMP for each of the processes and projects

9.3.2 Tasks
The steps of the RSKM process are described below:

9.3.2.1 Determine Risk Sources and Categories (SP 1.1)

The PM/ FH will determine the risk sources in the Project/SF and categories based on Risk repository, Contract, URS, SRS, Design document, project specific list of risks and list of risks during implementation and Create Risk List using TPRSM01 for the Project/SF.

9.3.2.2 Define Risk Parameters (SP 1.2)

The PM/FH will evaluate the risk occurrence and determine the probability, Impact of the occurrence and the threshold to trigger the management activities and will record the same in RMP using TPRSM01

9.3.2.3 Establish a Risk Management Strategy (SP 1.3)

The PM/FH will determine the scope of risk management effort; methods and tools used, Time and define measures to monitor the status of risk and its periodicity and will record in RMP using TPRSM01

9.3.2.4 Identify Risks (SP 2.1)

The PM/FH will identify the risks by examining the steps in WBS, Seeking expert opinion, Review of similar projects and lessons learned and will record in RMP using TPRSM01

9.3.2.5 Evaluate Categorize and Prioritize Risks (SP 2.2)

The PM /FH will evaluate identified risks using defined parameters and categories and prioritize risks for mitigation and will update RMP using TPRSM01

9.3.2.6 Evaluate Categorize and Prioritize Risks (SP 2.2)

The PM/FH will prepare mitigation plan and contingency plan for identified risks with the list of responsible people and will update the RMP. SM/BUH will review and approve the RMP and will create MOM(FRMMR02) or IRR (FRVER01).

9.3.2.7 Implement Risk Mitigation Plans (SP 3.2)

  • The PM/FH will monitor and track risk status and invoke selected risk handling options when monitored risk exceed the defined thresholds and collect performance measures on risk handling activities and will update the risk repository. PM, Team members and other stakeholders discuss among themselves and will prepare project list of risk and projected risks during implementation.
