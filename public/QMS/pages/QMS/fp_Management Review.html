Management Review
ISO Clause: 5.6 FP15 Version : 3.00 Date : 17th May 2010

15.1 Introduction

15.1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this procedure is to provide guidance to the senior management team to focus on:

  • Process Improvement
  • Defect Prevention
  • Usage of metrics for better execution of projects

15.1.2 Policy

This process will be applicable to review and monitor the effectiveness of the quality management system of the organisation as required by the ISO 9001:2000 standard.

15.1.3 Scope

This procedure will be applicable to all process and procedures of Integra.

15.2 Stakeholders

15.2.1. Responsibilities

Sl. No. Role Responsibility Authority

3. MR Schedule Management Review Meeting Maintain Management Review Records Nil

15.3 Task Details

15.3.1. Schedule of Management Review Meeting

Management Review Meeting will be held every six months.

15.3.2. System Review Meeting

The System Review meeting will review process improvements.

The Management Representative (MR) will organize the system review meeting at least once in six months. The Managing Director will chair the meeting and the participants will be MR, Director, EPG, DH, Internal Quality Auditors and other invited members.

The following table provides the responsibilities and the agenda for the System Review Meeting:

Responsibility Agenda
MR Report the pending issues of previous review related action items Status of Organization objectives (Metrics of Projects and Support functions). Results of Internal Audit - Major issues, corrective and preventive action taken for Projects and other Support functions. Analyze and report the findings based on Surveillance Audit Reports Customer Feedback (Customer Satisfaction Survey, Customer feedback / compliant to DH, Marketing, Architect and PM). Status of Corrective and Preventive Actions taken for specific issues (Issues other than internal audit). Changes that could affect the QMS (Projects in new domains, New portfolios/ line of business). Recommendations for Improvement (QIPs, Suggestions given in last SEPG meeting).
General Others (If any).
Invited Members Report findings based on current performance

15.3.3. Maintain Management Review Records

The MR will maintain the MOM of the System review Meeting using Minutes of Meeting (FRMMR02). The MR will maintain the review records (minutes and other presentation materials used in the review meeting) for at least three years.

Attendance of meetings will be kept using FRMMR01.
