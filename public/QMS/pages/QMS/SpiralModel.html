Spiral Model
ISO Clause: 7.1 PM04 Version: 3.00 Date: 17th May 2010

Introduction

The spiral software development model is characterised by repeatedly iterating elemental development process and managing the risk throughout. The process model was suggested by Boehm and hence also is known as Boehm model. The basic Boehm’s model is shown in the figure below.

For ease of implementation the process model is divided into four processes.

1. Plan

2. Risk analysis,

3. Engineering and

4. Customer evaluation.

The activities of the process model starts after the project initiation. The PM will prepare high level schedule for the project and call for the project kick-off meeting within one week of the project initiation. The project kick-off presentation will be used for the meeting. The project charter will be signed by all the stakeholders.

Cycle 1:

➣ The PM will document the project plan using PMP (TPPPL03), CMP (TPCON01), QAP (TPPPL04) and RMP (TPRSM01) templates.

➣ The Architect will analyse the project risks along with the PM and team members. – Refer Risk Management Procedure PR09

➣ All the identified risks and the mitigation plan for the same will be documented in the RMP (TPRSM01) .

➣ The Architect will prepare prototype/ POC to validate the requirements. (TP/GL)

➣ The Architect will prepare SRS TPRQD02.

➣ Review the SRS through peer reviewers using the Verification procedure PR07 and maintain the review record.

➣ The testing team will prepare the STP and the STS – Refer Validation procedure PR08.

➣ The Architect will review the STP and STS and maintain the review record FRVER01. Refer Verification procedure PR07

➣ The PM and the Architect will re-estimate the work based on the SRS. – Refer Project Planning Procedure PR02

➣ The PQA will carryout a review to ensure completeness of the activities of cycle 1.

➣ The PM will send the Prototype, SRS, STP and STS to the customer for approval.

Cycle 2:

➣ The PM will prepare CR FRCON03, if any and update the SRS TPRQD02, STP TPVAL01 and STS TPVAL02 based on the input given by the customer.

➣ Update the RTM TPREQ02.

➣ The PM will update the project plans*

➣ The PM and Architect will revisit the risk scenario of the project and update the RMP (TPRSM01).

➣ Prepare/update the prototype as necessary.

➣ Architect/PL will prepare the HLD using TPDES01. – Refer Technical Solutions Procedure PR06

➣ The test team will prepare the ITP (TPPRI01) and ITS (TPPRI02)

➣ The Architect/PL will review the ITP and ITS and maintain the review record FRVER01.

➣ The PM and the Architect will re-estimate the work based on the HLD. Refer Project Planning Procedure PR02

➣ The PQA will carryout a review using checklist CHSDM02 to ensure completeness of the activities of cycle 2.

➣ The PM will send the updated prototype (optional) and the HLD to the customer for approval.

Cycle 3:

➣ The PM will update the project plans*

➣ The PM will prepare CR FRCON03, if any and update the HLD (TPDES01) based on the input given by the customer.

➣ The PM and Architect will revisit the risk scenario of the project and update the RMP (TPRSM01).

➣ Update the prototype if required.

➣ The PL will prepare LLD (TPDES02). Refer Technical Solutions Procedure PR06

➣ The Architect will review the LLD and maintain the review record FRVER01.

➣ The team members will the develop code using the approved LLD. They should ensure that the appropriate coding guideline is adhered to. Refer Technical Solutions Procedure PR06

➣ The PL and peer team members will review the code and maintain the review record FRVER01. Refer verification procedure for detailed process.

➣ The team members will carryout unit test after completion of the code review. Refer PR06 Technical Solutions Procedure

➣ Unit test report will be maintained by the team.

➣ Team will fix the unit test bugs.

➣ Team will carryout UT defect analysis using CAR. The PQA will be present in the analysis.

➣ Effort for rework to fix the Unit test bugs will be measured.

➣ On successful completion of the UT, the development team will carryout integration testing and record the IT bugs.

➣ The development team will fix the integration test bugs, and performs the testing once again.

➣ IT bugs will be analysed using CAR. The PQA will be present in the analysis.

➣ Rework effort to fix the integration test bugs will be measured.

➣ On successful completion of the integration testing the testing team will conduct system testing of the software. Refer Validation Procedure PR08.

➣ All System Testing bugs will be recorded using BCC

➣ The development team will fix the bugs and the testing team will carry out the testing once again to check whether the bugs are fixed or not.

➣ Rework effort to fix the System test bugs will be measured.

➣ The team will conduct analysis of ST bugs using CAR.

➣ On completion of system testing release readiness review will be conducted by the PQA and the Quality Manager and BU head will authorise the delivery of the product to the customer.

➣ PL will prepare DN and RN in PCC.

➣ The PM will send the final product to the customer along with DN and RN.

Cycle 4:

➣ Based on the acceptance test report provided by the customer the PM will update the project plans.

➣ The team will update project document; SRS, HLD, LLD, STP, STS as necessary.

➣ The development team will make necessary bug fixes.

➣ The testing team will conduct regression testing. Refer Validation Procedure PR08.

➣ Customer reported bugs will be recorded in the BCC.

➣ The development and testing team and the PQA will carry out CAR for escaped defects.

➣ The PQA will conduct review prior to release of the software to the customer.

➣ The PM will ensure acceptance from the customer.

➣ The PM will release the resources and close the project. Project plan: The four documents viz PMP, CMP, QAP and RMP form the project plan.
