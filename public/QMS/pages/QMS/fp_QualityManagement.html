13.1 Introduction

13.1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this procedure is to:

  • Establish the organizational responsibilities for process improvement activities
  • Develop and maintain software processes of all the projects of the organization, and improve the processes to meet the quantitatively measurable goals.

13.1.2 Policy

This process will be applicable for documenting & maintaining the quality related documents & records of the organization.

13.1.3 Scope

This procedure will be applicable for all software projects and the support functions of Organization.

13.3 Task Details

A description of the tasks involved is given in the following sections.

13.3.1 Maintain the QMS of the Organization

The Management Representative (MR) will maintain the QMS as per the QMS Document Management (FP14) procedure. Any feedback against the Quality Management System by third parties will be logged in the format QMS Complaint Log (FRQMS03).

13.3.2 Training on QMS

The SQA, Management Representative will conduct training to all the employees on the Quality Management System. QMS Training will be a part of the Induction Program. All Lateral Hires will also be trained on the Organization’s QMS as per the Talent Management (FP05)procedure.

13.3.3 Processes tailoring

Based on the needs of the project or customer requirements, if the process for a project does not comply with the organization’s QMS fully or partially, the DH and PM will tailor the processes to suit the project’s needs and will be documented in the SPMP. Any process deviations will be analysed and approved by the SQA/MR

13.3.4 Plan and Conduct Internal and External Audits

The MR/SQA will plan the internal audits as per the Internal Audit (MP04) procedure. The MR/SQA will plan and organize the external audits and assessments with an external agency.

13.3.5 Periodic Review

Periodic Reviews of all the ongoing projects of the organization will be carried out to ensure compliance and effectiveness of the QMS. The objective of this is to complement the project groups in meeting the organizational quality objectives first time. The member of the SQA team will do this.

13.3.6 Metric Data Collection and Analysis

The SQA team will collect all metric data pertaining to the projects as stated in the Measurement and Analysis Procedure (FP11) Status Report.

13.3.7 Maintain the Process Database of the Organization

The SQA team will maintain the Organizational Process Database (OPD), which contains the organization’s process assets.

The PM will hand over to the SQA team, a copy of the approved SPMP document and estimates at the time of kick-off of the project.

During the Project Closure, will hand over the completely detailed SPMP document and estimates to the SQA

13.3.8 Processes improvement

The SQA will implement the process improvements identified during the audit process or on request. All the requests for improvements of the QMS can be sent to SQA department through email.

13.3.9 Customer Feedback

  • The SQA/MR will conduct customer satisfaction survey.
  • The DH will identify the list of external customers for survey. The survey will be done at least once in six months or at the end of the project delivery or more frequently on a need basis as decided by the delivery head.
  • The SQA/Manager - Quality will send the Customer Satisfaction Survey Form for Projects (FRQMS01) and for Consulting (FRQMS02) to the concerned customer representative and obtain the feedback.
  • Customer Satisfactory Survey data is analysed and presented for Management Review.
  • The MR will share the feedback with DH and PMs.

13.3.10 Status Reporting

The status reporting is done through Project Progress Review as and when planned by SQA, and twice in a year Management Review Meeting is conducted.

13.3.11 MLD and MLR

The SQA, Management Representative will maintain MLD and MLR for the function.

13.3.12 Metric collection and analysis

The Metrics will be collected as per Metric Plan in every quarter and analysed for corrective / preventive action.
