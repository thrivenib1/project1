Information and Work-flow
ISO Clause: 5.5.3 and 7.2.3 FP17 Version: 3.60 Date: 30th March 2015

17.1 Introduction

17.1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this procedure is to define the means and mechanism of information and work-flow in Integra so as to ensure an effective quality management system.

17.1.2 Policy

This procedure is defined to establish information flow across all levels of employees of the organization.

17.1.3 Scope

This procedure is applicable to the communication and work-flow between various functions within Integra and also with the customer.

17.3 Task Details

17.3.1. Library Services

17.3.1.1. Maintain Inventory of Books and QMS Manuals

The Library-in-charge will maintain the inventory of books and QMS Manuals (if maintained in hard copy). The Library-in-charge will do the physical verification of the inventory annually in the month of March.

17.3.1.2. Issue of Books and QMS Manuals

The Library-in-charge will control the issue of books and QMS Manuals. The books can be borrowed for a duration of two weeks. The borrower should return or get it re-issued after two weeks.

These procedures describe processes for all the activities involved in the production of iNEWS.

Tasks Owner Description References

 iNEWS Guidelines
Review In-charge, iNEWS

Deployment Editor
  1. Release the magazine on the last working day of the month.

17.3.3. Integra's Internal Website, PCC

Integra’s internal web site will include the following:

  • “iNews”: All the versions of Integra’s monthly newsletter
  • PCC: Project / Process database, Knowledge base, Tools
  • QMS: All the documents related to Integra’s quality management system
  • Facilities: Telephone numbers etc.
  • Library-contains information of books in library, on-line study materials and postings of articles.

This website is maintained by the CTO.

17.3.5. Conduct Employee Interactive Meet

The Employee Interactive meet is a forum for mass communication, where the business plan and objectives are communicated to the employees and the employees can give their feedbacks or raise their concerns to the management.

The management will organize the EIM once in a year. The Managing Director will chair the meeting and will be participated by all employees of Integra.

Responsibility Agenda
MD Report on Business Status and presentation of Business plan
MR General feedback from employees.
MD Question & Answer session

17.3.6. Communication and Work-flow

The work related communication would be generally as e-mail or notes (hard copy). Other general communication will be through common e-mails and/or postings in notice board.

Employees will ensure that all work related e-mail communications, both internal and external, will be done only using the Tuxedo or integramicro mail-IDs as allocated to them for the purpose.
