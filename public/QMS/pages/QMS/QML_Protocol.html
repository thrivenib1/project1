QMS-01

Enclosed herewith a copy of the above document, which is the Policy Manual of Integra. This forms the highest level in our Quality System documents. The following protocol shall be followed in using this document, which is the policy decision of Integra.

General

This manual is a controlled document. The manual has the approval of the Chairman/Managing Director. All amendments to the manual will be made with the approval of the Chairman/Managing Director. Suggestions from all employees for improvement of the quality system and the documents are welcome.

The document and its contents are for the internal use of Integra. Reproduction in any form by anyone of the material contained herein without the permission of the Managing Director or M.R. of Integra is prohibited.

Hard Copy

The custodian of the controlled copy is responsible for keeping it up-to-date by incorporating the amendments promptly.

The custodian is also responsible for:

  • Handing over this copy to the person taking charge in case of reallocation of duties, transfer, retirement or resignation.
  • Making available the manual for reference to the concerned person working in his area.

MR may distribute "Uncontrolled Copies" of the Quality Manual to external agencies. These copies will not be updated. Integra will periodically review and revise the quality system as stated in section 4.1 of this manual.

All those who are issued with copies are requested to identify the location where the document shall be preserved and inform the users working with them and the M.R. for systematic utilization and updating of the document. This is a mandatory requirement of ISO 9001:2008 standard.

Soft Copy

The soft copy of this document is available in our Intranet site for reference of all the employees of Integra. Please login to the PCC to access the QMS.
