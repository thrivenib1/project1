Integra Quality Manual

The Quality Management System of Integra has two main sections. In the first section we have the Quality Manual, the Procedures and Process Models for the projects. In the second section, under the work aid, you will find the Guidelines, Coding Standards, Templates, Checklists and Forms. To access the work aids please go to the work aid section in the main page. All projects and functions will follow the mandatory procedures of Control of Document, Control of Record, Internal Audit, Corrective and Preventive Actions and Continual Improvement process. The projects will also comply with Non-compliance procedure.
