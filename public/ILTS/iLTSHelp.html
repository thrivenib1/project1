Online Help

Personal Leave Information

This displays the information about the number of personal leaves that an employee can avail. The number of casual leaves and privileged leaves available for the employee for the calendar year is displayed.

Requesting for Leave

On click of the "Request Leave" button opens up the leave application form. On submission of the Leave Application form a mail will be sent to supervisor, yourself and HRD. The supervisor needs to approve the leave request for making the emplyoyee eligible for the leave.

Reapplying for Leave

This gives the details about the pending leaves (leave requests not yet accepted by the supervisor) for an employee. If the supervisor requests the employee to reapply, those leave requests would be listed under pending leave requests section for the employee. On selecting any of the pending requests listed here, the employee will be required to re-submit the leave application form after modifying the previous leave request details if needed . The leave request could also be deleted using the "Delete" button if the employee wishes to cancel the leave request.

Leave Approval (Applicable for supervisors only)

Here the login person ( or the supervisor ) who has been requested for leave by the other employees, can approve the leave request using "Approve" button or can reject the leave request using "Reject" button or can ask the employee who has requested for leave to reapply the request if needed.

Personal Leave History

It displays the detailed history about the approved leaves and rejected leaves for the employee.

Employee Contact Information

This gives the contact information about the employee during the leave period.

Employee Leave History

It displays the detailed history about the approved leaves and rejected leaves for the employee who has requested leave.
